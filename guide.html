Getting Started

Join FloCraft for the first time and find yourself in a cavern deep underground. Check out the FloCraft guides in your inventory. Walk through the cave to the Start Portal to begin your FloCraft Odyssey. The portal will send you to a random spot in the wild! From here, the world is yours to explore.

General Commands

This is a compiled list of general commands that may be useful when playing on the FloCraft server.
Toggle PvP
/pvp on|off
Players' Nametag will appear red when PvP is enabled.
Coordinates HUD
/coordinates toggle
Toggles HUD which includes XYZ coordinates, directionality, and time (actual time, not game time).
Books
/getbook <name>
Learn more about the server in-game by retrieving the server books. Replace <name> with Commands, Info, MOTD, and Rules.
Mail
/mail [send/clear/read] [playerName] (message)]
Use send accompanies with a player name to send a player mail. Clear deletes your inbox, and read allows you to read your mail.
Message
 /msg [player]
Sends a private message to the player entered.
Ping
/ping (player)
Shows a targeted player's ping.
Top Players (By Play-Time)
/playtimetop
Shows a list of the top players ranked by total play time.
Play-Time Rewards
/prewards
Check the remaining time left before you receive your next play-time reward.
Ranks
/rankinfo (rank) | <player>
Shows information for each rank or view rank information for a player. 
/ranklist
Shows all possible ranks.
Sign Shift-Edit
/cmi toggleshiftedit
Toggle sign shift-edit on or off.
Just For Fun
/sit
Use this command to have your player take a seat or get up from sitting. 
/hat
Use the item you currently have in hand as a hat!

Homes

Homes are a way for players to teleport to a saved location through the use of beds. Place a bed and crouch right-click it the set your Home. To teleport, type /home in chat.

Ranks

FloCraft has ranks based on time played.

Novice - New Players
Apprentice - 30 Minutes
Artisan - 1 Day
Expert - 1 Week
Master - 1 Month

Keys & Crates

Keys can be found randomly in crates

+1 adventure key every hour played
+1 vote key for each vote with /vote
+1 daily key every day with /daily

Land Claim

Land claim creates an unraidable area, free from grief and theft. Blocks can’t be broken and containers are locked. Doors, trapdoors, and fence gates are all locked. Button and lever use is locked. Pressure plates are not locked.

Create a claim by typing /claim
Delete a claim with /abandonclaim
Allow players to build in your claim with /trust
Remove a players trust with /untrust

Claim Tool: Golden Shove (Insert picture) Use the golden shovel to select 2 points and make a claim Inspection Tool: Stick (Picture) Use a stick to get info about a claim. Players receive 100 claim blocks per hour played

Skills

Skills are a unique was to show your progress on the server. As you level up your skills, you gain attributes related to each skill. Some attributes are double drops, bleed, less fall damage, and more.

More info can be found by using /<skill>

https://mcmmo.org/wiki/Main_Page)

Parties

Parties Team up with your friends to gain benefits as you level up your party. Share experience, teleport to each other, and party chat are some of the features

/party help - shows commands

Trade

Easily and safely trade with other players through the Trade GUI. Use /trade for help Request a trade with /trade (name) Accept a trade with /trade accept (name) Place your items on the left side of the inventory. You will see the other players inventory on the right. Click the red block to initiate the trade Click the block again to confirm the trade
